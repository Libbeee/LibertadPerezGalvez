About Me

Born and raised in a small Guatemalan town, Libertad Perez-Galvez discovered her passion for creativity through video games and drawing during her childhood years. At age 13, she and her mother relocated to the United States, marking a significant transition that opened new opportunities while allowing her to maintain her cultural connections. Though embracing life in America, Libertad cherishes her Guatemalan heritage, particularly through its vibrant cuisine, whose distinctive flavors remain an irreplaceable part of her identity. Currently pursuing her Bachelor's Degree in Interactive Digital Media & Marketing at the College of Westchester, she balances her academic studies with a part-time job that develops her professional and social skills. Her creative ambitions focus on the digital entertainment industry, specifically in animation, film production, and video game development, where she hopes to apply her skills in digital artistry, interactive media creation, and strategic marketing to forge a successful career in the creative field.
